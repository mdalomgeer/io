About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher with a robust background in Data Science, specializing in machine learning (ML), deep learning (DL), natural language processing (NLP), and large language models (LLMs). My work spans multiple fields, including health informatics and predictive modeling, with a strong focus on harnessing AI to solve real-world challenges.

In my current research, I leverage large health datasets to develop patient-centered AI solutions. I'm building conversational agents using fine-tuned LLMs to provide accessible health information for underserved communities. Additionally, I have experience in advanced NLP and ML techniques, including time-series analysis and graph neural networks.
