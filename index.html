I am a Computer Science PhD Researcher at UMBC, focusing on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Computer Vision (CV). My broad research is on Decomposition-based Reasoning using text or vision data, with a current focus on question-based decomposition.

My past work includes Outcome and Intention based Decomposition for video-text retrieval and Event Representation Learning for text summarization, question answering, and counterfactual reasoning (published in *SEM 2023, ACL).
